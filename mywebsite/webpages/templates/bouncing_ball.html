Bouncing Ball

Fill out the following form to create your own customized bouncing ball. However, before doing so, let's review a few things from high school physics.

  1. Movement of the ball is modeled by two equations: x = x0 + vxt and y = 1/2at2 + v0yt + y0 where values of x0, vx, v0y, and y0 are entered in the form
  2. As the above mentioned equationos show, there is only acceleration in y direction and its value is constant (a = 2)
  3. Whenever the ball hits the horizontal walls or vertical walls, it's vx or vy is multiplied by a fraction factor (0.9)
  4. For x axis and y-axis positive directions are rightward and downward, respectively
